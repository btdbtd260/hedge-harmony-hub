

# Hedge Trimming Company Management App

A clean, professional internal operations tool for managing your hedge trimming business — starting as a frontend prototype you can expand later.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Dashboard
- Overview of today's scheduled jobs, upcoming work, and key stats (total customers, pending quotes, revenue this month)
- Quick-action buttons to create a new job, quote, or customer

### 2. Job Scheduling
- Calendar view (weekly/monthly) showing all scheduled trimming jobs
- Each job shows customer name, address, assigned crew, and status (scheduled, in progress, completed)
- Create/edit jobs with date, time, location, service type, and crew assignment
- Job status tracking workflow: Scheduled → In Progress → Completed

### 3. Customer Management
- Searchable list of all customers with name, phone, email, and address
- Customer detail view showing contact info, service history, and linked quotes/invoices
- Add/edit customer records

### 4. Quoting & Invoicing
- Create quotes with line items (service type, quantity, price)
- Convert accepted quotes into scheduled jobs
- Generate invoices from completed jobs
- Status tracking: Draft → Sent → Accepted/Declined for quotes; Unpaid → Paid for invoices

### 5. Crew Management
- List of crew members with contact info and role
- View crew availability and current job assignments
- Assign/reassign crews to jobs

---

## Design
- Clean, professional look with neutral tones and a subtle green accent color
- Sidebar navigation for quick access to all sections
- Responsive layout (works on desktop and tablet)
- Data stored locally in the browser for now — ready to connect to a database later
